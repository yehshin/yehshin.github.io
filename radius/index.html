Radius - Mobile UI Project

Radius is a personal project I created to help people visually save money.

The project was designed using Figma.



The Idea

The idea of the project is to help people visibly keep track of how much they are spending.

After the user either spends or earns money, they would enter the amount and tap the screen. When they do, a circle will form where the size corresponds to the amount they spent or saved.

The Goal

The goal for the design is that users will not only be able to easily keep track of their spendings, but to visibly see it as well.

The purpose of the applicaiton is to provide an overall impression to the user by the majority color of the screen, indiciating if they have been spending too much or too little, while still providing the exact amount at the top of the screen.
