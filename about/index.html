Who am I

My name is Ye Heon Shin, but people call me Yay. I'm a recent grad from New York University.


I originally went to NYU to go into law, which is what I studied for the first three years. However, my final year, I switched to design to see what loving your work is like.


It feels amazing.


A quote that stuck with me when I heard it on a documentary regarding the light effects used in Star Trek was made by one of the CGI artists.



"You don't notice it when it's there, but do when it's not."



This is a concept I try to implement into my designs where users can mindlessly and seamlessly use the product with clear-cut execution. I appreciate the subtle aspects that build up the entire product. My goals with design are to increase inclusivity, improve quality of life, and to be constantly learning along the way.


I'm currently seeking a design internship, so if I seem like a good fit for your team, please send me an email!
