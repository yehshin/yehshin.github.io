Hey there, my name is Ye Heon Shin. I'm a recent NYU alum and am currently living in West Bloomfield, Michigan.

I'm not a designer by trade. I actually went to NYU with the intent of going into law, which is what I did for the first three years there. However, my final year I realized I was studying it for the wrong reasons and decided to go into development and design.

It was then when I found out what enjoying your work meant.

1) I am on Netflix in "Bill Nye Saves the World" episode 8 (that's me in the middle smiling really awkwardly)
