My name is pronounced "Yay"

Which is what I try to embody wherever I go.

I'm a designer and recent alum of New York University. I attended NYU with the intent of going into law, but switched to design my final year to see what loving your work meant. It feels amazing.

A quote that hasn't left my mind since I heard it on a documentary about Star Trek (Star Wars is better) was made by one of the CGI designers in regards to the light effects used in the movies. He said, "it's something you don't notice when it's there, but do when it's not." I try to implement this concept into my designs in a way where users can mindlessly and seamlessly use the product with clear-cut execution. I find myself especially fascinated with the motion and interactive aspects of design. My goals with design are to increase inclusivity, make daily living easier, and to be constantly learning along the way.

I'm currently seeking a design internship, so if my work impressed you and I sound like a good fit for your team, please send me an email!
