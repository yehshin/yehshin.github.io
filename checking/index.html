Betterment Checking

Designed checking account for both web and native platforms

Overview

Betterment has been serving customers with their investments for years. People have been able to easily manage their portfolios and watch their money grow.

After working in this realm for a while now, there was a desire to expand opportunities. The opportunities to allow people manage not only their investments, but all their finances easily in one place.

Cash management came to light and Checking became the initiative to lead it.

Outcome

Full public launch in April 2020, we definitely weren’t expecting to celebrate over zoom away from each other, but nonetheless were able to release the product to all our Betterment customers and prospects.
