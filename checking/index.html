Betterment Checking

Designed checking account for both web and native platforms

Overview

Betterment has been serving customers with their investments for years. People have been able to easily manage their portfolios and watch their money grow.

After working in this realm for a while now, there was a desire to expand opportunities. The opportunities to allow people manage not only their investments, but all their finances easily in one place.

Cash management came to light and Checking became the initiative to lead it.

Outcome

Full public launch in April 2020, we definitely weren’t expecting to celebrate over zoom away from each other, but nonetheless were able to release the product to all our Betterment customers and prospects.

So why checking?

There were several reasons why Betterment wanted to break into cash management:

  1. People’s relationship with investing is different with their cash, and being able to consolidate that for everyone would allow better financial management
  2. While different assets, there are still overlaps in the relationship with cash and investing, such as money movement and advice that can help people people be more efficient with their money
  3. Current banking solutions are tedious and brick-n-mortar, and we saw an opportunity to provide a much better experience, all while being online

How we built a checking account

While a big focus was in allowing consolidation of having cash and investments in one place, we also wanted to make sure the checking account could stand proudly on it’s own as an account worth being the primary.

Being online only, we wanted to make sure card and account management were easy to get to. Customers can easily activate their card from the app as well as lock it or change their PIN.

We also made it easy for customers to manage their transactions with smart and custom categorizations, date filtering, and exporting for their budgeting needs. We also provide a direct deposit form that is online generated for them to give to their employer.

Customers can also easily upload checks from their phone to deposit into their account with mobile check deposit.

We also partned with Dosh so customers can earn cashback rewards on their spending from merchants they can search for right in the app. We also highlight their cumulitive rewards earned and highlight the deposit timelines so customers can easily keep track of those. They can also easily keep track of ATM Fees reimbursed, as that is another offering we provide, along with reimbursements on international fees and no hidden account fees and mobile wallets.

Customers can also create a joint checking account with their partner by simply adding them to their existing account or creating a new one. They’ll have shared and equal access to the account so they can manage their finances together.

They can also easily set up their cash to sweep into their savings or other Betterment accounts with Two-Way Sweep, which allows for automatic money movement based on the account balances. We’ll optimize the optimal amount for each account so you can earn the most from your money.
